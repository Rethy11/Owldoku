Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 19, 2026

Plasticine Games (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information is and is not collected when you use Owldoku and other Plasticine Games websites and applications.

By using this website, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Who We Are

Plasticine Games is a small independent game studio owned and operated by Shawn.Genji as a sole proprietorship based in Tennessee, United States.

Information Stored on Your Device

Owldoku stores certain information locally within your web browser using Local Storage.

This information may include:

  • Music and sound effect volume preferences
  • Gameplay settings
  • Personal best scores
  • Completed achievements
  • Puzzle progress
  • Other game preferences that improve your experience

This information remains on your device only.

Plasticine Games cannot view, collect, or access this information.

You may delete this data at any time by clearing your browser’s site data or local storage.

Information We Collect

Plasticine Games does not intentionally collect personal information such as:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Home address
  • Phone number
  • Payment information
  • Government-issued identification
  • User accounts or passwords

Owldoku does not require creating an account.

Analytics and Tracking

At this time, Plasticine Games does not operate its own analytics platform.

We do not intentionally collect gameplay analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics.

Cookies

Plasticine Games does not intentionally place tracking cookies for its own purposes.

However, third-party services integrated into the website, such as advertising providers, may use cookies or similar technologies as described in their own privacy policies.

Advertising

Owldoku may display advertisements provided by Google AdSense or other advertising partners.

These services may collect information about your device or browsing activity in accordance with their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

Plasticine Games does not control how these third-party services collect, process, or use information.

For more information, please review Google’s Privacy Policy.

Third-Party Services

The website may utilize third-party services including, but not limited to:

  • Google AdSense
  • GitHub (for source code hosting)
  • Instagram (for social media links)

Each third-party service maintains its own privacy practices and policies.

Children’s Privacy

Plasticine Games does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

If you believe personal information has been submitted by a child, please contact us and we will make reasonable efforts to address the issue.

Data Security

Because Plasticine Games stores gameplay information locally on your own device rather than on our servers, much of your gameplay data never leaves your browser.

While we strive to use reputable hosting providers and maintain secure development practices, no website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Clear your browser’s local storage at any time.
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings.
  • Use browser features that block advertising cookies.
  • Install privacy extensions or content blockers if you prefer.

Please note that removing local storage may erase saved preferences and game progress.

International Visitors

Plasticine Games operates from the United States.

If you access this website from another country, your use of the website is governed by applicable United States laws, subject to any mandatory protections provided by your local jurisdiction.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as Owldoku and Plasticine Games evolve.

Any significant changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date at the top of this page.

Continued use of the website after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact Plasticine Games through Instagram: @plasticinegames.
